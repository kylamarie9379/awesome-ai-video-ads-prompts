## 📋 Tips for Better Video Ads

Effective ads require specific elements. Use these guidelines when building your content:

* **The Hook:** The first three seconds matter. Use a prompt that starts with a strong question or a visual surprise.
* **The Body:** Keep the middle section focused on the benefits of your product rather than the features. 
* **The Call to Action:** Always finish by telling the viewer what to do next. Use phrases like "Shop the link in bio" or "Join our community today."
* **UGC Elements:** Authentic videos perform well. Encourage creators to film real feedback rather than scripted ads.
